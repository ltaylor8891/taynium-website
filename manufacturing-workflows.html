Manufacturing & Digital Workflows

Connecting CAD, production data and the systems around them.

Disconnected engineering information is one of the most common — and most expensive — problems in manufacturing businesses.

01 — PRODUCTION DATA

Drawing, DXF & Production-Data Management

Taynium works on drawing issuing, DXF generation, flat-pattern exports, revision tracking, production metadata, drawing-to-part traceability, material and thickness validation, and controlled reissue processes.

COMMERCIAL VALUE
  • Reduced manual administration
  • Fewer incorrect files reaching production
  • Better revision traceability
  • Faster drawing release
  • More consistent naming and properties
  • Less reliance on individual memory
  • Stronger foundations for ERP or PDM integration

Manufacturing Systems & Digital Workflow Development

TYPICAL WORK
  • Requirements studies and workflow mapping
  • Data-structure planning

  • Integration feasibility studies
  • Internal software specifications and prototype systems
